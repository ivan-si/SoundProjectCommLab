Image Gallery with Audio Descriptions

Heartbeat

With a little bit of editing, the sound of a foot tapping on the carpet was a good base for the thumping of a heart

Heartbeat sensor

Using the beep of the printer, we recreated the mechanical beeps of a heart rate monitor.

Guitar (acceptance)

With the help of our friend Sanchith, we recreated the riff from the song Pluto Projector which is used in different forms throughout the stages of grief, symbolizing the protagonist’s development.

Drums

The sound of banging drums perfectly captures the feeling of anger, progressively getting faster and messier

Piano (depression)

Singular, dissonant notes are used as the backdrop to various stages, eventually leading to the iconic melody from the song Runaway as acceptance nears

Scribbling

The sound of a pen scribbling furiously against paper creates a sense of anxiety and desperation that is representative of the bargaining stage

Pillow scream

Screaming into a pillow during fits of frustration is something that everyone can relate to, making it the perfect sound to represent the stage of anger
